Your first week after certification

  1. Complete Module 7 sandbox submission — if not already posted, finish the issue and share the URL with your lead.
  2. Get lead sign-off on the certification checklist (all six items).
  3. Graduate the sandbox issue to the live board when approved — Backlog first, then Build only when hypothesis and kill criteria are locked.
  4. Join the weekly Build Loop review — move cards honestly, record Learn decisions with evidence, enforce the three-active WIP cap.
  5. Browse deepening material — how-to/ and videos/ as they publish; skill deep-dives under skills/.

Forking for another product

Keep Modules 1–6 as the portable core — no product-specific URLs in the main flow. Swap the appendix links only: live board URL, canonical issue template on the product repo, and week-1 experiment drafts. Teaching mirror stays in this repo at .github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/build-loop-experiment.md.

Portable rule: If a teammate learning on Product B has to read Product A’s board to finish Module 4, the core course is too coupled. Product pointers belong in the appendix.
