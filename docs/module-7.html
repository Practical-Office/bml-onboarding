Hard truth

SOP on the live issue — what to do

  1. Pick + approve — choose one live backlog card; lead confirms WIP ≤3 and sequencing. Teaching drafts below are shape examples only — sharpen the live issue body, do not copy-paste.
  2. Assumption — refine hypothesis on the issue from your Module 1 sentence.
  3. Ticket — all six sections on the live issue; numeric pass/kill in Measure.
  4. Backlog — experiment label; stay in Backlog until hypothesis + kill criteria are written.
  5. Build — move to Build; run /grill-with-docs → /to-spec → /to-tickets → /implement (/tdd + /code-review inside implement; tiny builds may skip to /implement).
  6. Ship smallest test — module gate: Build in progress or first PR merged.
  7. Stretch: Measure — move to Measure; post week-0 baseline on the issue.

Document column moves and weekly numbers as comments on the live issue. Learn → Done is ongoing board ops — see next steps.

Ticket 4 — PricingModule 6 walkthrough

H: Customers pay $399+/mo after 60 days. B: Renewal script + structured feedback at day 45–60. M: renewal without heavy discount · kill on strong price pushback. L: Hold price, adjust packaging, or pilot tier — start only at renewal window.
